Our mission

What drives us

Wie alles begann

Our foundation for selling coffee was already laid through the cultivation of cacao in Peru. Under the name "Meybol Cacao Germany GmbH," I have earned a trustworthy reputation among Peruvian women and men farmers because I work with them transparently and honestly using fair, humane, and ecological means.

Gemeinsam Gutes tun

No matter whether you enjoy our coffee or our chocolate specialties, behind the name Meybol you will find a philanthropic and ecologically valuable company that stands up for its producers, pursues high quality standards, and at the same time does good for nature.

Starke Frauen, starker Kaffee

We do not sell our coffee for profit, but out of the conviction to make the world a little better, step by step. Through our trade, we aim to protect the rainforest, counteract coca cultivation, and advance the empowerment of Peruvian women farmers.
