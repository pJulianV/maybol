Our partnerships

We are not alone.

To make the sale of our coffee possible and pursue our big goals, we cooperate with various partners who support us on our journey and help us move forward.

Cooperations

We cooperate with

Frauenorganisation aus VRAEM – Peru

The women of Sumac Suncu are the true guardians of the VRAEM rainforest. They strictly ensure that villagers do not use chemical fertilizers and fight for sustainable agriculture without coca in the VRAEM region.

Genossenschaft Ashaninka & Nomatsiguenga

The goal is to improve the quality of life and living standards of their members through knowledge and education. Their main activity is ecological farming, applying cooperative principles of solidarity and mutual assistance.

Meybol Cacao Germany GmbH

Laborious handwork makes this tree-to-bar chocolate an exclusive product. The cacao farmers are paid a fair price so they and their families can live well and practice ecological agriculture at the same time.
