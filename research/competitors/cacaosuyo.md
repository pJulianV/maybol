# Cacaosuyo (cacaosuyo.pe)

**Key features & observations:**

- Strong Peruvian heritage messaging: "best kept secret of the Incas".
- Awards: multiple International Chocolate Award golds; emphasize origin-specific bars.
- Detailed "Sobre nosotros" and "¿Qué hacemos?/¿Cómo lo hacemos?" sections highlighting traceability, collaboration with small farmers, quality focus.
- Products: coverage for various cacao origins, chocolate categories, ceramic etc.
- Contact form and social media links in footer.
- Legal pages and newsletter prompt.

**Inspiration:**
- Deep origin storytelling and regional breakdowns.
- Highlighting sustainability through farmer partnerships.
- Prominent awards as trust signals.
